About Us

Engineering Discipline, Built Into Every Project.

We bring civil engineering rigor to construction management — protecting your budget and timeline through daily site discipline, not paperwork after the fact.

Project Management Consultancy, Built for Real Sites

Weston Engineers is a project management consultancy based in New Town, Rajarhat, Kolkata. We work across residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects throughout West Bengal, bringing site supervision, quality control, and technical advisory to every stage of construction.

Our approach is simple: catch problems while they're still cheap to fix, verify every bill against work actually done, and keep contractors and timelines coordinated — so our clients aren't the ones discovering issues after the concrete is already poured.

✦ How We Work

From Site Assessment to Sign-Off

01

Site Assessment & Planning

We review drawings, scope, and site conditions before a single contractor is mobilized.

02

Contractor Coordination

03

Daily Quality Monitoring

Inspection and defect tracking happen on-site, every day — not in a report after the fact.

BOQ and quantity surveying confirm every bill matches work actually completed.
